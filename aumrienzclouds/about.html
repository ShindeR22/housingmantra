Who We Are?

Aumrienz Cloud Technologies is a leading provider of Salesforce solutions, specializing in consulting, CRM customization, AppExchange development, cloud advisory, and corporate training.


Founded by Kanika Chauhan, a Salesforce expert with 10+ years of experience, we empower businesses across finance, healthcare, retail, and tech with tailored, innovative solutions.


Our mission is to drive digital transformation through scalable and future-ready Salesforce implementations, ensuring customer success at every step.

Salesforce Consulting & Implementation

Aumrienz Cloud Technologies provides expert Salesforce consulting and implementation services to help businesses streamline their CRM operations.

AppExchange Development & Support

We specialize in AppExchange app development, offering end-to-end solutions from design to deployment and ongoing support.

Salesforce Support & Maintenance

Our team provides dedicated Salesforce support and maintenance services, ensuring smooth CRM operations and system efficiency.

Outsourcing & Staff Augmentation

We offer expert Salesforce professionals for outsourcing and staff augmentation to enhance your workforce and project execution.

Corporate Salesforce Trainings

Our corporate Salesforce training programs equip businesses with the necessary skills and expertise to maximize their CRM investments.

Individual Trainings & Career Guidance

Product Development

We assist businesses in developing and optimizing Salesforce-powered products tailored to their unique needs.
